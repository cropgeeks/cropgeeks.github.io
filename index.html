Our Principles

Openness

Where not limited by third-party licenses, we strive to make all our code open-source and available to everyone on GitHub.

Portability

It's important to be able to run software on as many devices as possible. Most of our tools are therefore web-based or have cross-platform support.

Community

We actively support our community of users by running training courses and interacting on social media and GitHub issues.
